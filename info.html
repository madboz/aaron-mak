About this site

Like Aaron's work in fashion, the website's form and concept are influenced by his own experiences and identity.

Special features include live footage of Aaron's family farm; seasonal color palettes; an anecdotal Camera Roll section that depicts banal yet influential moments in his daily activities; and custom UI elements and icons that reference musical notation and Chinese imagery.
